Need Help? We're Here!

Having trouble? Found a bug? Just want to share how much you love the app? Whatever's on your mind, we're all ears!

While we can't promise to solve every problem (we're only human, after all), we do promise to:

  • Read every message you send
  • Try our very best to help
  • Respond with actual human words (no robots here!)
  • Keep it friendly and simple
✉️

Send Us a Note

We'll help if we can – and we usually can!

Email Us

We typically respond within 24 hours

Before You Write

To help us help you faster, it's super helpful if you can:

  • Tell us what device and iOS version you're using
  • Describe what you were doing when the issue occurred
  • Include screenshots if you have them (they're worth a thousand words!)

Emergency?

If something's really wrong (like, "the app is posting cat videos without your permission" wrong), add URGENT to your email subject line. We'll bump you to the front of the line!

Feature Requests

Got an idea to make the app even better? We love hearing those! While we can't promise to implement every suggestion, we do promise to read and consider each one. Sometimes the best features come from users like you!
